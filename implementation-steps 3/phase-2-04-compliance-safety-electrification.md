# Phase 2 Step 4: Compliance, safety, and electrification

**Goal:** Polish compliance, safety, and electrification so they are visible and clearly differentiated for the investor demo. Emphasize UK/EU depth and AI-driven optimization.

**Prerequisites:** Phase 2-01 through 2-03 done.

---

## 1. Compliance visibility

- Compliance items: list with filters (type, status, expiring within N days). Link to related entity. Compliance at risk dashboard card links to filtered list.
- MOT, V5C, insurance: clear document type labels; link to documents/RAG where applicable.

---

## 2. Safety and H and S

- Vehicle checks: list by vehicle, date, status; show page with checklist. Optional link to defects.
- Risk assessments, toolbox talks, PPE, permit to work, safety observations: all under Compliance and risk or Safety in nav. Link from driver show to wellness and coaching.

---

## 3. Electrification and fleet optimization

- Electrification plan: Generate returns clear result (EV readiness, recommendations, charging). Show in card or table.
- Fleet optimization: Analyze returns right-sizing, utilisation, TCO. Present clearly. Both linked from dashboard or nav under Fuel and energy or Sustainability.

---

## 4. AI job runs and analysis results

- AI job runs list: type, status, entity, date; show page with result summary. Link to entity.
- AI analysis results: list or Recent AI insights widget (type, entity, finding, priority). Click to entity or recommendation view.

---

## 5. Done when

- Compliance and at risk easy to find; safety modules grouped in nav; electrification and optimization produce visible results; AI runs and results visible.
- Proceed to **phase-2-05** for investor demo narrative and polish.
